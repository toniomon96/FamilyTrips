# Logan / Trusted Planner Handoff

Use this when a trusted friend or family member is ready to create a real trip or event.

## What Toni Sends

```text
Here is the planner: https://thegroupchat.voyage/trips/new

Use this edit PIN: [send the shared edit PIN separately]

Choose Build my plan, paste everything you already know, and then answer the follow-up questions if it asks any. The draft is better when you include the messy context: stay/resort, dates, travelers, arrival/departure timing, must-dos, food preferences, budget/pace, anything already booked, and anything you definitely do or do not want.

After the preview looks close, hit Accept and it will create a private unlisted link you can share. You can keep editing the trip from the manage page with the same PIN.
```

## What Makes A Good Draft

For trips, ask them to include:

- Stay name and address or resort/city.
- Flight, drive, check-in, and checkout timing if known.
- Travelers, kids/ages, mobility needs, food preferences, and budget style.
- Must-dos in plain language, such as "golf one morning," "horseback riding on the beach," or "Lovers Beach, but not too packed."
- Anything already booked and anything that still needs a reservation.
- Preferred pace: loose, balanced, or packed.

For events, ask them to include:

- Event type, venue/location, guest count, date, and timing.
- Food/drink plan, supplies, setup/cleanup needs, and helpers.
- Must-have moments like cake, game setup, group photo, speeches, or rides.
- Constraints like kids, weather, parking, accessibility, budget, or quiet hours.

## Privacy Reminder

Do not paste passports, payment cards, private confirmation numbers, or anything that should not be shared with the group. Unlisted links are casual share-link privacy, not full account security.

## After The Draft

- Review the itinerary or run-of-show first.
- Check every item marked `needs-booking` or `needs-confirmation`.
- Add exact confirmation numbers privately outside the shared planner unless everyone should see them.
- Use Smart Assist from the manage page later to fill gaps, add booking reminders, or make the plan looser/tighter.
